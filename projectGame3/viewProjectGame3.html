The Calculator Game

Here's a preview of my 'Calculator Game' project.
This interactive game, crafted with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, provides an enjoyable and educational experience for testing your math skills.
What sets this game apart is its ability to save your test results to local storage. Your progress and performance in various math exercises are automatically stored, allowing you to track your improvement over time.
