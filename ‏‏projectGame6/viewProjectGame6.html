Grocery List

Here's a preview of my 'Grocery List' project.
This interactive application, developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, provides a convenient and user-friendly experience for managing your grocery shopping list.
You can create, update, and organize your shopping list with ease, and the changes are automatically stored in the local storage for your convenience.
